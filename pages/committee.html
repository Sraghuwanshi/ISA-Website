What is ISA and what are the differenet roles and their responsibilities

Indian Student Association

ISA is Indian student association formed by Indian Students in USU for Indians. The role of ISA is to maintain and leverage the indian traditions and cultures in USU.

The main responsibility of Cultural Secretary is to bring new cultural and traditional initiatives for various festivals celebrated at USU. The cultural members work in a team with cultural secretary to bring something new to platform.

The PR team takes care of establishing the valuable public relations and marketing for events.

The web master is the admin of the website and manages everything related to website and social media accounts of ISA.
